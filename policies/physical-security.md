# Physical security

## Version history

| Version | Effective date | Owner | Summary |
|---------|----------------|-------|---------|
| 1.0 | April 10, 2026 | Engineering | Initial documented physical security policy. |

## Purpose

This policy defines physical access controls for Grunt AS premises to protect personnel, equipment, and information.

## Scope

This policy applies to all Grunt AS office locations and areas where company equipment or information is accessed or stored.

## Access controls

| Control | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Office access | Access restricted to authorized personnel via key, access card, or equivalent |
| Visitor access | Visitors must be accompanied by an employee at all times |
| Equipment areas | Server rooms or areas with sensitive equipment have restricted access |

## Workplace practices

- Employees must lock their workstations when leaving them unattended
- Confidential documents must not be left visible or accessible in shared or public areas
- Company devices must be secured when not in use, particularly when working outside the office

## Device handling

Lost or stolen devices must be reported immediately so that access can be revoked and the device wiped remotely if applicable.

## Review

This policy is reviewed annually and updated based on changes to office locations or operational requirements.
