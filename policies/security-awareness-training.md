# Security awareness training

## Version history

| Version | Effective date | Owner | Summary |
|---------|----------------|-------|---------|
| 1.0 | April 10, 2026 | Engineering | Initial documented security awareness training policy. |

## Purpose

This policy ensures that all Grunt AS employees stay informed about security and privacy practices relevant to their work.

## Scope

This policy applies to all Grunt AS employees, including full-time, part-time, and contractors with access to company systems or data.

## Training program

All employees are required to complete security and privacy awareness training on an annual basis.

### Topics covered

Training covers the following areas:

- Phishing and social engineering awareness
- Password and authentication best practices
- Data classification and handling (per the data management policy)
- Acceptable use of company systems and devices
- Incident reporting procedures
- Physical security awareness
- Privacy and data protection obligations

### Delivery and frequency

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Frequency | Annually |
| Format | Online training module or equivalent |
| New employees | Must complete training within 30 days of joining |
| Updates | Additional training may be provided when significant security changes or incidents occur |

## Completion tracking

Training completion is tracked and recorded. Employees who have not completed their annual training are followed up with by management.

## Review

This policy is reviewed annually and updated to reflect changes in the threat landscape, security practices, or regulatory requirements.
