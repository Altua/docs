# Customer notification procedure

## Version history

| Version | Effective date | Owner | Summary |
|---------|----------------|-------|---------|
| 1.0 | April 10, 2026 | Engineering | Initial documented customer notification procedure. |

## Purpose

This procedure defines how Grunt AS notifies customers of material changes to services, security practices, or company ownership.

## Scope

This procedure applies to changes that could materially affect how customers use Grunt AS services or the security of their data.

## Events requiring notification

Customers will be notified in advance of:

- Material changes to the functionality or availability of services
- Significant changes to security practices or data handling
- Changes to company ownership or corporate structure
- Changes to terms of service or data processing agreements
- Security incidents that affect customer data (per the incident response process)

## Notification process

| Step | Details |
|------|---------|
| 1. Identify | Engineering or management identifies a change requiring notification |
| 2. Draft | A clear notification is drafted describing the change, its impact, and any actions required by the customer |
| 3. Approve | Notification is reviewed and approved by management or CTO |
| 4. Deliver | Notification is sent to affected customers via email |
| 5. Record | The notification and its distribution are documented |

## Timing

- Planned changes: Customers are notified with reasonable advance notice
- Security incidents: Customers are notified as soon as practicable after the incident is confirmed and assessed

## Roles and responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|---------------|
| Management / CTO | Approve notifications, determine scope of distribution |
| Engineering | Provide technical details for notifications |

## Review

This procedure is reviewed annually and updated based on changes to services or regulatory requirements.
