The legacy support guidance for macOS focuses on the official uninstaller rather than manual file deletion.

## Remove Grunt from a Mac

1. Download the official Grunt uninstaller from the support workflow.
2. Open the downloaded uninstaller from your **Downloads** folder.
3. Run the uninstaller and follow the prompts.
4. Restart the Mac after the uninstall completes.

## Why use the uninstaller

The legacy support article recommends the bundled uninstaller so Grunt files are removed in the supported order without manual cleanup.

If you need a reinstall after removal, use the current installer from [app.grunt.pro/download](https://app.grunt.pro/download).
