## Purpose

This policy defines controls for email security at Grunt AS, covering email filtering, spam protection, and restrictions on the use of personal email for business purposes.

## Scope

This policy applies to all Grunt AS employees and contractors who use email in connection with their work.

## Email filtering and spam protection

All company email accounts are protected by email filtering and spam services that:

- Filter incoming messages for spam, phishing, and malicious attachments
- Block known malicious senders and domains
- Quarantine suspicious messages for review

## Personal email restrictions

- Company business must be conducted using company-provided email accounts
- Personal email accounts must not be used to send, receive, or store company data or confidential information
- Access to personal email on company devices is permitted but must not be used for business purposes

## Employee responsibilities

- Report suspicious emails to the security team or management
- Do not open attachments or click links from unknown or suspicious senders
- Do not forward company information to personal email accounts

## Review

This policy is reviewed annually and updated based on changes to email infrastructure or emerging threats.
