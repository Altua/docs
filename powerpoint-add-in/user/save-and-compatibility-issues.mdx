title: "Save and compatibility issues"
description: "Check Office and Grunt architecture alignment when save errors or instability appear"
---

If PowerPoint shows a save error while Grunt is installed, one possible cause from the legacy support guidance is an architecture mismatch between Microsoft Office and the Grunt add-in.

## Why architecture matters

If Office is `32-bit` and Grunt is `64-bit`, or the other way around, you can see:

- Save failures
- Crashes
- Unexpected behavior
- Lower stability

## Check your Office architecture

1. Open PowerPoint or another Office app.
2. Open **File**.
3. Choose **Account**.
4. Look under **Product Information** to confirm whether Office is `32-bit` or `64-bit`.

## Check your Grunt architecture

1. Open the **Grunt** tab.
2. Open **Settings**.
3. Open the information icon at the bottom of the settings view.
4. Check whether the Grunt add-in is `32-bit` or `64-bit`.

## Fix the mismatch

If the architectures do not match:

1. Download the correct Grunt installer from [app.grunt.pro/download](https://app.grunt.pro/download).
2. Install the matching build.
3. Restart PowerPoint.

If your organization controls deployment centrally, ask IT to distribute the matching package instead.
