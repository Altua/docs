---

## Grunt tab is missing

1. Confirm Grunt is installed from [app.grunt.pro/download](https://app.grunt.pro/download).
2. Restart PowerPoint.
3. In PowerPoint, check whether the add-in is disabled/inactive and re-enable it.
4. If it still does not appear, reinstall Grunt.

## License expired or not activating

1. Open **Grunt** > **Settings** > **License**.
2. Re-authenticate.
3. Restart PowerPoint and try again.

If your company uses a proxy/firewall, contact IT to verify required Grunt endpoints are reachable.

## Generate diagnostics for support

Use the built-in troubleshooter to create a support zip and share it with your admin or Grunt support.

For article-level help, open [support.grunt.pro](https://support.grunt.pro/).
